About Game Unleashed:

Welcome to Game Unleashed, a leading tech company specializing in game development for PC, macOS, and Linux. With a rich history dating back to 1978, we have been at the forefront of the gaming industry, delivering innovative and captivating experiences for gamers worldwide.

Our Mission:

At Game Unleashed, our mission is to bring back the joy and nostalgia of retro gaming. We believe that the games we played when we were kids hold a special place in our hearts, and they deserve to be celebrated and cherished. With our deep understanding of the gaming landscape and a passion for reviving classic titles, we are committed to delivering unforgettable gaming experiences that transport you back to those cherished moments.

Reviving Break/IT:

In 1979, Game Unleashed released its first video game, Break/IT. This brick breaker game took the gaming world by storm, captivating players with its addictive gameplay and simple yet challenging mechanics. Originally developed for the Fairchild Channel F console, Break/IT became an instant classic and left a lasting impact on gamers of that era.

Embracing the Growing Retro Gaming Market:

With our finger on the pulse of market trends, we recognize the increasing popularity of the retro gaming universe. As enthusiasts ourselves, we understand the joy of revisiting the games that defined our childhood. As a result, we have made it our strategic priority to revive and reintroduce classic titles like Break/IT to the modern gaming landscape.

Break/IT 8:

Today, we proudly present Break/IT 8, a modern adaptation of the iconic brick breaker game that started it all. Remastered for PC, macOS, and Linux platforms, Break/IT 8 combines the nostalgia of the original with enhanced graphics, intuitive controls, and exciting new features.

In Break/IT 8, you will embark on a journey filled with ball-bouncing action, brick destruction, and challenging gameplay. Strategically move the paddle to bounce the ball and demolish bricks of varying colors and lives. Catch bonuses that provide unique power-ups, including the ability to multiply your firepower, temporarily glue the ball to the paddle, unleash unstoppable fireballs, and control the speed of the game.

Immerse yourself in the classic visuals and addictive gameplay that made Break/IT a beloved retro title, all while enjoying the convenience and capabilities of modern gaming platforms.

Join us on this nostalgic adventure and experience the magic of Break/IT 8, as we pay homage to the games that shaped our gaming history.
